---
title: OCR & text recognition
description: Read text on screen with embedded Tesseract or optional PaddleOCR.
---

OculiX ships Tesseract **embedded** through [Legerix](https://github.com/oculix-org/Legerix). No manual install. Bundled languages: `eng`, `fra`, `spa`, `chi_sim`, `hin`, `osd`.

For high-accuracy CJK or complex layouts, install the optional [paddleocrserver-powered](https://pypi.org/project/paddleocrserver-powered/) server.
